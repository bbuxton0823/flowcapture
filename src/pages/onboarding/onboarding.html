Welcome to FlowCapture

The SOP capture tool built for HACSM

FlowCapture helps you create step-by-step instructional manuals for Yardi and other systems. Click through any workflow and FlowCapture automatically captures each step with a screenshot.

Annotate Highlight, arrow, blur PII

Step 1: Capture a Workflow

1
Click the FlowCapture icon in your browser toolbar (top-right). The popup opens.
2
Name your SOP in the text field at the top (e.g., "Move-In Certification Process").
3
Click "Start Capturing" — the button turns red and shows a step counter.
4
Navigate to Yardi (or any site) and click through each step of the process. Each click automatically captures a screenshot.
5
Click "Stop Capturing" when you've finished the workflow.
Tip: You'll see a purple ripple and a small toast notification each time a step is captured. The badge on the extension icon shows how many steps you've recorded.

Step 2: Edit & Annotate

After capturing, click "Edit" in the popup to open the full editor. Here you can:

Reorder steps — drag and drop to fix the sequence
Edit titles and descriptions — give each step a clear name and detailed instructions
Annotate screenshots — add highlights, arrows, text boxes, and blur/redact PII (Social Security numbers, names, etc.)
Delete steps — remove any accidental or duplicate captures

Step 3: Agency Notes & Role Tags

FlowCapture is built for HACSM's specific needs. Two powerful features help customize SOPs for your agency:

Agency Notes

Add callout notes to any step to highlight HACSM-specific instructions. Four note types are available:

Info — General HACSM guidance
Warning — Important: don't skip this
Critical — HUD compliance requirement
Tip — HACSM shortcut or best practice

Role Tags

Assign each step to a specific audience so the right people see the right instructions:

All Staff Housing Manager Front Desk Finance

When exporting PDFs or videos, you can filter by role so each team gets a tailored version.

Step 4: Export PDF & Video

PDF Export

Print-ready manuals with HACSM branding, agency notes, role badges, and approval status. Perfect for binders, email distribution, and Yardi LMS document storage.

Video Export

Narrated instructional videos (1080p MP4) with chapter markers and closed captions. Upload to Vimeo, then paste the link into Yardi's LMS for on-demand training.

About narration: For final videos with audio, use the ElevenLabs Pro Voice tier in the video settings. This is the only option that embeds clear narration directly into the MP4 file. The "Speaker Preview" option reads narration through your speakers for testing but does NOT include audio in the exported file.

Sharing SOPs

Use the green Export SOP / Import SOP buttons in the popup to share .flowcapture files with your team. The SME captures the workflow, then the PCO or manager imports it to review, add agency notes, and set the approval status before final export.
